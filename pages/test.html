Ayushman Bharat Yojana

Providing healthcare benefits to the underprivileged sections of society.

Eligibility Criteria

Income-based Criteria

Families with an annual income less than ₹5 lakh are eligible for the scheme.

Family Type Criteria

Rural and urban families meeting the criteria set by the government can apply.

Documents Required
  • Valid ID proof
  • Income certificate
  • Address proof
