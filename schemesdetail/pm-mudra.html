Om Mudra Yojana Overview

The Om Mudra Yojana is a government initiative to provide financial assistance to small and medium businesses through affordable loans.

The scheme aims to provide financial support to the youth, women, and entrepreneurs from various sectors such as manufacturing, retail, and service industries.

The loan is offered in three categories: Shishu, Kishore, and Tarun, based on the stage of the business, with interest rates as low as 7%. These loans are aimed at making it easier for individuals to start or expand their business ventures.

Key Benefits

Low Interest Rates
  • Loans available at an interest rate of 7% per annum
  • Flexible repayment options
Easy Access
  • Available for small businesses, youth, and women entrepreneurs
  • Minimal documentation and fast approval
Support for Growth
  • Helps in business expansion and growth
  • Provides working capital for day-to-day operations
