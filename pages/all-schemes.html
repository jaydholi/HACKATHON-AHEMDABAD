Government Welfare Schemes

Explore various welfare schemes offered by the Government of India

PM Kisan Samman Nidhi

Financial assistance of ₹6,000 per year to farmer families

PM Awas Yojana

Housing for All - Urban and Rural housing scheme

Ayushman Bharat

Health insurance coverage up to ₹5 lakhs per family per year

PM Ujjwala Yojana

Free LPG connections to women from BPL households

Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana

Savings scheme for girl child education and marriage

PM Mudra Yojana

Loans up to ₹10 lakhs for small business ventures

PM Jan Dhan Yojana

Financial inclusion program with zero balance accounts

PM Kisan Samman Nidhi
Eligibility Criteria:
  • All landholding farmer families
  • Family must have cultivable landholding
  • Both rural and urban farmers are eligible
Required Documents:
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Land Records
  • Bank Account Details
  • Recent Passport Size Photograph
PM Awas Yojana
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Family should not own a pucca house
  • Annual income should be within specified limits
  • Should not have availed housing benefit before
Required Documents:
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Income Certificate
  • Bank Account Details
  • Property Documents (if any)
PM Ujjwala Yojana
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Woman of BPL household aged 18 and above
  • No LPG connection in the household
  • Must be a resident of India
Required Documents:
  • Aadhaar Card
  • BPL Certificate
  • Bank Account Details
  • Proof of Address
Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Girl child below 10 years of age
  • Account can be opened by parents/legal guardian
  • Only one account per girl child (maximum two accounts in case of twins)
Required Documents:
  • Birth Certificate of Girl Child
  • Parent's/Guardian's ID Proof
  • Address Proof
  • Passport Size Photograph
PM Mudra Yojana
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Any Indian citizen with a business plan
  • Age 18 years and above
  • Non-defaulter to any bank/financial institution
Required Documents:
  • Identity Proof (Aadhaar/PAN)
  • Business Plan/Proposal
  • Address Proof
  • Bank Account Statement
  • Business Registration (if applicable)
Ayushman Bharat
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Families included in SECC database
  • Previously covered under RSBY
  • No regular formal employment in family
  • Belongs to identified deprivation categories
Required Documents:
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Ration Card/PDS Document
  • Income Certificate (if applicable)
  • Proof of Address
  • Family Member Details
PM Jan Dhan Yojana
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Indian citizen aged 10 years and above
  • No other bank account (for zero balance account)
  • Valid KYC documents
  • Both rural and urban residents eligible
Required Documents:
  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card (if available)
  • Proof of Address (Voter ID/Driving License/NREGA Card)
  • Recent Passport Size Photograph
  • Mobile Number (for SMS alerts)
