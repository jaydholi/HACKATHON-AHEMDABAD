Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana Overview

A scheme to empower the future of a girl child with financial security.

Eligibility
  • Only for a girl child
  • Age of the girl should be less than 10 years
  • Indian citizens only
  • Any number of accounts can be opened for each girl child
Benefits
  • High interest rates (7.6% annually)
  • Tax benefits under Section 80C
  • Flexible deposits ranging from ₹100 to ₹1.5 lakh annually
  • Loan facility against the balance after 3 years
Required Documents
  • Birth certificate of the girl child
  • Parent's identity proof (Aadhaar, Voter ID, etc.)
  • Proof of residence (electricity bill, ration card, etc.)
  • Bank passbook for opening the account
