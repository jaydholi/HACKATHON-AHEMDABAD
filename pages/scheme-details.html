PM Kisan Samman Nidhi

Empowering farmers with financial assistance

Scheme Overview

The PM Kisan Samman Nidhi provides direct financial support to farmers in India. Eligible farmers receive ₹6,000 annually in three equal installments of ₹2,000 each. The funds are directly transferred to the beneficiaries' bank accounts.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Small and marginal farmers with cultivable land up to 2 hectares.
  • Farmers with valid Aadhaar-linked bank accounts.
  • Exclusions: Institutional landowners, retired professionals, and tax-paying farmers.

Benefits

  • Provides income support to farmers to meet agricultural and household needs.
  • Encourages investment in farming equipment and practices.
  • Reduces reliance on informal credit sources.

Application Process

  1. Visit the official PM Kisan portal: pmkisan.gov.in.
  2. Click on "New Farmer Registration" and fill out the form with the required details.
  3. Submit the form and track your application status on the portal.

Contact Information

For assistance, contact the PM Kisan Helpline: 155261 or email at pmkisan-ict@gov.in.
