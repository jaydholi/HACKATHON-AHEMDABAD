PM Ujjwala Yojana Overview

The PM Ujjwala Yojana aims to provide LPG (Liquefied Petroleum Gas) connections to households living under the poverty line, empowering them with a cleaner and safer cooking method.

The government has launched this scheme to improve the health and well-being of women and children by reducing the health risks posed by traditional cooking methods such as wood, coal, and biomass.

Under this scheme, LPG connections are provided to eligible households free of cost, along with financial assistance for the purchase of an LPG stove and refills. This initiative is expected to significantly impact rural households across India.

Key Benefits

Cleaner Cooking
  • Reduces the use of firewood, coal, and other traditional fuels
  • Promotes a healthier environment for women and children
Empowerment
  • Empowers women with a safer and more efficient cooking method
  • Improves the overall quality of life for families
Rural Development
  • Helps reduce rural dependence on unsafe cooking fuels
  • Improves economic productivity by saving time and health costs
