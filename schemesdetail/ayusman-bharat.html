Scheme Overview

Here’s a breakdown of the eligibility, benefits, and required documents to apply.

Eligibility
  • Indian citizens
  • Annual family income below ₹5 lakh
  • No existing health insurance coverage
Benefits
  • Free health treatments at recognized hospitals
  • Coverage for critical illnesses up to ₹5 lakh annually
  • Access to both public and private health facilities
Required Documents
  • Proof of Identity (Aadhaar/Passport)
  • Proof of Address (Electricity Bill/Bank Statement)
  • Income Certificate (Family Income Details)
