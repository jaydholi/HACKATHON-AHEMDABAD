Income Assistance Program

Get the support you need with our financial assistance program.

Eligibility Criteria

To apply for the income assistance program, you must meet the following criteria:

  • Resident of the region for at least 6 months.
  • Annual income must be below $30,000.
  • Not receiving any other government assistance.
  • Need temporary financial support due to unforeseen circumstances.
