PM Awas Yojana Overview

PM Awas Yojana is a government initiative to provide affordable housing to families from economically weaker sections, low-income groups, and middle-income groups, in both urban and rural areas.

The main goal of this scheme is to ensure "Housing for All" by 2022. Under this scheme, the government provides financial assistance to eligible families for constructing or buying a house, improving the quality of life.

The government also offers subsidies on interest rates for home loans and aims to provide safe, secure, and sustainable housing solutions for the economically disadvantaged groups.

Key Benefits

Affordable Housing
  • Government provides financial assistance for construction or purchase of homes.
  • Affordable interest rates on home loans.
Beneficiary Groups
  • Beneficiaries from economically weaker sections (EWS), low-income groups (LIG), and middle-income groups (MIG).
  • Special focus on women and marginalized communities.
Social Welfare
  • Promotes inclusive growth and social welfare.
  • Reduces homelessness and improves living standards.
