PM Kisan Yojana Overview

Providing income support to farmers across India.

Eligibility
  • Indian Farmers
  • Farmers cultivating up to 2 hectares of land
  • No income from other government schemes
  • Farmers must be registered with government schemes
Benefits
  • ₹6,000 per year in 3 equal installments
  • Direct transfer to farmer’s bank account
  • Financial support for crop production and other expenses
Required Documents
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Bank Account Details
  • Land Ownership Certificate
  • Farm Registration
